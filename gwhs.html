#Current projects (GWHS)

Global wheat head segmentation dataset

The Head Segmentation (GWHS) dataset aims at an instance segmentation of spikelets, including their damage by biotic and abiotic stresses

The dataset targets a cascade of several objectives:

  • Detect fully visible spikes in a canopy
  • Segment and count spikelets per spike
  • Detect and quantify diseases (such as fusarium head blight)
  • Quantify effects of abiotic stress (such as heat, frost, or shading)

To achieve this we aim to label a dataset enabling to:

  1. detect fully visible spike including their orientation to the camera
  2. segmentation of ALL spikelets within a head in order to count them and determine their size
  3. quantify damage related to change in size, color and shape of the spikelets

The targeted image are:

Red-green-blue (RGB) images
At least 0.4 mm/pixel ground sampling distance (40 pixels per spike width)
